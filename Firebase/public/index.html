Together at Home


During this pandemic, our world has stopped, We have nothing to do, nowhere to go and no one to visit. Due to this, people are suffering from mental health problems like stress, depression, anxiety etc.


Idea of our App

The idea of creating this app is to help people to overcome loneliness, stress and anxiety. Together at home emphasis on creating events, participating in events and viewing schedule for events. This app also includes social media feature which helps to connect people.
