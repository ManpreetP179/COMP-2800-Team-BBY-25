About our Website

Together at Home focuses on combating mental health issues which are faced by people who are in self isolation due to Covid-19 pandemic. We would help people by connecting them to other people so that they can share their feelings and experiences with other. The features of our application are:

1.Creating online events
2.Schedule of events
3.Connecting people through social media.
